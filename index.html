About Heritage Sourcing

Heritage Sourcing specializes in acquiring and reselling authentic luxury pieces from globally recognized houses such as Christian Louboutin, Ralph Lauren, Tom Ford and much more.

Each item is carefully sourced through trusted channels, with a focus on authenticity, condition, and exclusivity. Rather than mass retail, we offer a more personal approach — connecting clients with sought-after pieces that are not always easily available locally.

From statement footwear to refined essentials, every request is handled with discretion, precision, and attention to detail. Whether you're looking for something specific or open to what's available, we're here to help you acquire it.
